About Bunion3D

We believe every foot is unique — and effective correction must be, too. Bunion3D combines AI-driven modelling with on-demand 3D printing to deliver personalised bunion correctors. By designing for scale, we aim to make clinical-level personalisation accessible and affordable.

How it works

1) Smartphone scan

We capture foot geometry and landmarks from a short guided scan — no special hardware required.

2) AI-driven modelling

Algorithms estimate deformity angles and generate a parametric CAD model tailored to your anatomy.

3) Skin-safe 3D print

A flexible, biocompatible device is 3D-printed and finished for comfort and progressive correction.

Understanding Hallux Valgus

Bunion3D’s approach considers key anatomical measurements such as the Hallux Valgus Angle (HVA) and Intermetatarsal Angle (IMA) to guide personalised device parameters.

  • Input: smartphone scan + clinical metrics (when available)
  • Output: parametric model tuned to your anatomy
  • Goal: progressive correction with comfort
